Focaccia whith Caramellized Onion, Cranberries, and Brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramellized onions, tart cranberries, and creamy brie. It will become your new favourite holiday starter!

Image: Sally Vegas

Fresh Cranberry Recipes That Go Beyond Sauce

Ingredients

    For the focaccia

  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 3/4 cups (350g) likewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan

    For the topping

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoon olive oil, for brushing the top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes (scrape ring off a paring knife if desired, though it's not necessary)
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dough

    2. In a medium-sized mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix together all the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed. You don't need to actually knead the dough, though I sometimes knead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate the last of the flour...
